Usage:

    reg <activity>

Explanation:

    Register activity.

Argument:

    activity: Optional.
              Name of ctivity you want to register, in Chinese.
              If not specified, we will register the first
              available activity for you.

Example:

    >>> reg 第三空间清水河校区综训馆
    报名成功

    >>> reg 第三空间沙河校区体育场
    已签到该活动
