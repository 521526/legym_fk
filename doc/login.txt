Usage:

    login [username] [password]

Explanation:

    Login your Legym account.

Argument:

    username: Compulsory.
              Your Legym username.

    password: Compulsory.
              Your Legym password.

Example:

    >>> login 18912341234 123456
    电子科技大学的XXX，欢迎回来！

Note:

    Username and password are compulsory arguments.
    But if you accidentally forget to type them in,
    we will also require your input again afterwards.
